Request Account Deletion – QRCL

This page explains how to request that your QRCL account and all associated personal data be permanently deleted.

Developer: QRCODENG LIMITED
App: QRCL

How to request deletion

1. Send an email

Write to us at Contact@qrcl.ng with the subject line “Account Deletion Request”.

2. Include your account details

In the email, provide the email address registered on your QRCL account. This is required for us to locate your account.

3. Confirmation

You will receive a confirmation reply within 30 days. Once confirmed, your account and all personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems, except where we are legally required to retain certain information.

What data is deleted

  • Your profile information (name, email, phone number, apartment ID, estate ID, property details)
  • Any uploaded images (profile, incident evidence, event images)
  • Incident reports, visitor records, and event history tied to your account
  • Your marketplace listings and store information

What data may be retained and for how long

  • Financial / transaction records: If applicable, retained for up to 7 years as required by Nigerian tax law.
  • Anonymous usage statistics: Data that has been anonymized so that it can no longer be linked to you may be kept indefinitely for analytics.
  • Security logs and legal claims: Limited access logs may be retained for up to 24 months after deletion for fraud prevention or to defend legal claims.

Contact

If you have any questions, email us at Contact@qrcl.ng.
